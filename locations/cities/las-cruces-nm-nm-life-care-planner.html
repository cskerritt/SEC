Life Care Planning Methodology

Our CLCP-certified approach ensures comprehensive and defensible life care plans that meet New Mexico court standards:

  • Medical Record Review: Thorough analysis of all medical documentation and prognosis
  • Physician Collaboration: Consultation with treating physicians and medical specialists
  • Needs Assessment: Identification of all future medical, therapeutic, and equipment needs
  • Cost Research: Current Las Cruces area pricing for all recommended services and items
  • Present Value Analysis: Economic calculations for lifetime cost projections
