Los Angeles Economic Overview

Los Angeles, the second-largest U.S. metropolitan economy with a GDP exceeding $1 trillion, offers diverse employment opportunities across entertainment, technology, aerospace, and international trade. Our vocational assessments account for LA's unique economic landscape and industry-specific earning potential.

  • Metropolitan Population: 3.9 million (city), 13.2 million (metro area)
