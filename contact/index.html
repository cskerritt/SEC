Frequently Asked Questions

How much does an economic analysis cost?

Fees vary based on case complexity, timeline, and scope of work. We provide detailed fee estimates after the initial consultation.

What is your typical turnaround time?

We typically provide preliminary economic assessments within 3-5 business days. Complete reports generally require 2-3 weeks, depending on case complexity and document availability.

Are you available for rush cases?

We accommodate urgent cases when possible. Rush analyses may require additional fees but we understand the time-sensitive nature of litigation.

Do you travel for depositions and trials?

Yes, we provide expert testimony services throughout New England. Video testimony is also available when appropriate.

What documents do you need to begin?

Typical documents include medical records, employment records, tax returns, and case pleadings. We'll provide a specific document list after understanding your case needs.

Can you critique opposing expert reports?

Yes, we regularly provide rebuttal analyses of opposing economic expert reports, identifying methodological issues and calculation errors.
