Expert Business Turnaround Services for New England Companies

When your business is in crisis, you need experienced guidance fast. We specialize in turning around distressed businesses throughout New England, helping companies navigate financial challenges and return to profitability.

Warning Signs Your Business Needs Turnaround Help

  • Consistent monthly losses or declining revenues
  • Unable to meet payroll or vendor obligations
  • Bank lines maxed out or in default
  • Tax liabilities mounting
  • Key customers or employees leaving
  • Considering bankruptcy or closure

If you're experiencing any of these, act now. Early intervention dramatically improves recovery chances.

Our Turnaround Process

Phase 1: Crisis Stabilization (Week 1-2)

  • Cash flow assessment and immediate preservation
  • Critical vendor and creditor communication
  • Stop the bleeding - eliminate unnecessary expenses
  • Secure short-term financing if needed
  • Stabilize operations and workforce

Phase 2: Assessment & Planning (Week 2-4)

  • Comprehensive financial analysis
  • Operational efficiency review
  • Market and competitive assessment
  • Develop turnaround strategy
  • Create 13-week cash flow projection

Phase 3: Restructuring (Month 2-3)

  • Negotiate with creditors and lenders
  • Restructure debt obligations
  • Rightsize operations and workforce
  • Renegotiate leases and contracts
  • Implement cost reductions

Phase 4: Recovery & Growth (Month 3+)

  • Execute revenue improvement initiatives
  • Rebuild customer relationships
  • Strengthen financial controls
  • Restore profitability
  • Position for sustainable growth
