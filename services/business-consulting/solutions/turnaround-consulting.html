Turnaround Consulting FAQs

Can my business be saved?

Most businesses can be saved if action is taken early enough. Key factors include: available cash or credit, market demand for products/services, management commitment, and time before critical deadlines. We provide honest assessments within 24 hours.

Will I lose control of my business?

No. We work as advisors, not managers. You retain full control and make all decisions. We provide expertise, negotiate on your behalf, and implement improvements, but you remain the owner and decision-maker.

How much does turnaround consulting cost?

Turnaround consulting typically ranges from $15,000-$75,000 depending on company size and complexity. Many clients use success fees or contingent payment structures. The cost of not acting is usually far greater - bankruptcy costs average $50,000-$200,000 plus business loss.

Should I just file bankruptcy?

Bankruptcy should be the last resort. It's expensive, damages credit for 7-10 years, may result in personal liability, and often means losing the business. 85% of our clients avoid bankruptcy through proper turnaround management.
