Comprehensive Vocational Assessment Services

Earning Capacity Analysis

Comprehensive evaluation of pre-injury and post-injury earning capacity, considering education, work history, skills, and labor market conditions.

  • Work history and career progression analysis
  • Education and skill assessment
  • Labor market wage research
  • Present value calculations

Employability Assessment

Detailed analysis of vocational abilities, transferable skills, and realistic employment options based on functional limitations and market conditions.

  • Functional capacity evaluation review
  • Transferable skills analysis
  • Job market accessibility research
  • Accommodation requirements assessment

Vocational Rehabilitation Planning

Development of practical rehabilitation plans including training needs, job placement services, and return-to-work strategies.

  • Training and education recommendations
  • Job placement assistance planning
  • Return-to-work timeline development
  • Cost analysis for rehabilitation services

Labor Market Analysis

In-depth research of regional employment opportunities, wage data, and industry-specific job availability for accurate assessments.

  • Regional job market research
  • Industry wage and benefit analysis
  • Employment trend evaluation
  • Occupational outlook projections

Our Vocational Assessment Process

1. Initial Case Review

Comprehensive review of medical records, employment history, educational background, and case-specific factors to understand the scope of assessment needed.

2. Individual Assessment

Direct evaluation through structured interviews, vocational testing, and functional capacity review to establish baseline abilities and limitations.

3. Labor Market Research

Detailed analysis of local and regional employment opportunities, wage structures, and job availability in relevant occupational categories.

4. Economic Analysis

Calculation of pre and post-incident earning capacity, incorporating transferable skills, realistic employment alternatives, and economic factors.
