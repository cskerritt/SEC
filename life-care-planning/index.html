Our Life Care Planning Process

1. Medical Record Review

Comprehensive analysis of medical records, treatment history, and prognosis to understand current condition and future care needs.

2. Provider Consultation

Collaboration with treating physicians, therapists, and other healthcare providers to validate care recommendations and treatment protocols.

3. Cost Research

Detailed market research of medical costs, equipment prices, and service rates from multiple providers to ensure accurate pricing.

4. Economic Analysis

Present value calculation of lifetime care costs, considering inflation rates, replacement schedules, and life expectancy factors.
