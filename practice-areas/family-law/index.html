Expert Financial Analysis for Divorce & Family Law

Complex divorce cases require sophisticated financial analysis to ensure equitable distribution of marital assets and appropriate support calculations. Our forensic economics team provides comprehensive financial expertise for high-asset divorces, business valuations, and income determinations that withstand scrutiny in family court proceedings.

  • Income Analysis: Determining true income for support calculations, including unreported income
  • Asset Tracing: Identifying and valuing marital vs. separate property
  • Lifestyle Analysis: Historical spending patterns for support determinations
  • Employability Assessment: Vocational evaluations for imputed income calculations
