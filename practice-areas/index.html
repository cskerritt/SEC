Economic Expertise Across Key Legal Practice Areas

Comprehensive economic damage analysis and valuation services tailored to the specific requirements of personal injury, medical malpractice, employment, and commercial litigation throughout Rhode Island, Massachusetts, and New England.

Skerritt Economics & Consulting provides attorneys with reliable, court-tested economic analysis across diverse practice areas. Our expertise spans the full spectrum of economic damages, from calculating lost earnings in personal injury cases to valuing businesses in shareholder disputes. We understand that each practice area has unique requirements, statutory considerations, and precedents that must be carefully addressed.

Our approach combines rigorous economic methodology with practical litigation experience, ensuring that our analyses meet court reliability standards while remaining accessible to judges and juries. Whether you're handling a complex medical malpractice case requiring life care planning or an employment dispute involving front and back pay calculations, we provide the economic foundation your case needs.
