About This Tool

Calculations Provided

  • Life Expectancy (LE): Remaining years of life based on current age and demographics
  • Work Life Expectancy (WLE): Expected remaining years in the workforce
  • Years to Final Separation (YFS): Time until permanent workforce exit

Based on standard actuarial tables and BLS workforce participation data.

Data Sources & Methodology

  • CDC/NCHS Life Tables (2021 base)
  • BLS Workforce Participation Rates
  • Standard actuarial adjustment factors
  • Education-specific work life patterns

Calculations follow forensic economics best practices.

Academic References
